# Ironmark Digital

Engineering-led digital infrastructure for the UK fire & life-safety sector.

Ironmark Digital develops structured compliance and operational platforms designed for real-world engineering environments. Our focus is on building reliable, secure systems that support maintenance providers, principal contractors, and property portfolios across the UK.

We prioritise:

* Structured data and traceable records
* Practical deployment in live compliance environments
* Secure architecture and controlled access
* Long-term maintainability

Our work is grounded in the operational realities of UK fire and life-safety compliance.

---

## Sentinel Register

Sentinel Register is our core platform.

It is a structured digital logbook and asset compliance system built specifically for UK fire maintenance contracts.

The platform provides:

* QR-driven access to systems and individual assets
* Structured digital fire logbooks
* Asset register capability
* Service and maintenance tracking
* Defect recording and lifecycle management
* Clear, time-stamped compliance records
* Site and portfolio-level deployment

Sentinel Register is designed to function as the digital backbone of compliance across operational property environments.

## FireSecure UK

FireSecure UK operates under the Ironmark Digital brand as a technical knowledge and engineering support platform for the UK fire & life-safety industry.

It consists of:

### wiki.firesecure.uk

A structured technical reference covering:

* UK fire alarm standards and regulations
* Smoke control and life-safety systems
* Installation practices and design principles
* Engineering explanations and regulatory context

### tools.firesecure.uk

A suite of practical engineering utilities, including:

* Compliance and design calculators
* Electrical and life-safety reference tools
* Structured technical utilities for on-site and design-stage use

FireSecure UK is designed to support engineers, designers, and contractors with clear, technically grounded reference material and practical calculation tools.
