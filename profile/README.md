# Ironmark Digital

Engineering-led digital infrastructure for the UK fire & life-safety sector.

Ironmark Digital develops structured compliance and asset management platforms designed for real operational environments. Our focus is on building reliable, secure systems that support maintenance providers, principal contractors, and property portfolios across the UK.

We prioritise:

* Structured data and traceable records
* Practical deployment in live engineering environments
* Secure architecture and controlled access
* Long-term maintainability

Our work is grounded in the realities of UK fire and life-safety compliance.

---

## Sentinel Register

Sentinel Register is our core platform.

It is a structured digital logbook and asset compliance system built specifically for UK fire maintenance contracts.

The platform provides:

* QR-driven access to systems and individual assets
* Structured digital fire logbooks
* Asset register capability
* Service and maintenance tracking
* Defect recording and status management
* Clear, time-stamped compliance records
* Site and portfolio-level deployment

Sentinel Register is designed to function as the digital backbone of compliance across operational property environments.
